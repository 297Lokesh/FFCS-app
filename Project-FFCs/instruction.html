Important Instructions Before FFCS Registration

  • Course Structure: At VIT, each course is defined using:
    • T - Theory
    • P - Project
    • L - Lab
    Please check all components carefully (e.g., T/P/L) before choosing a course.
  • Project Registration: If you choose a project-based course (P), you must complete the work under the guidance of the specific faculty you selected at the time of registration. Choose wisely.
  • Slot Selection: When selecting a theory and lab course, try to assign one in the forenoon (FN) and the other in the afternoon (AN). This prevents slot clashes and improves your daily schedule.
  • Credit Limits: You must register for a minimum of 16 credits and a maximum of 27 credits. Plan based on your academic load and CGPA.
  • Course Prerequisites: Check the prerequisite conditions before choosing electives or advanced courses. Ensure you've completed any required subjects beforehand.
  • Registration Priority: FFCS registration time slots are assigned based on CGPA. Be ready during your allotted window, as courses may get filled quickly.
  • Time Table Review: After registration, go to VTOP and confirm that your timetable has no slot clashes or errors.
⚠️ You cannot change or withdraw a course after submission unless within the add/drop window. Choose carefully and avoid last-minute confusion!
