Important Instructions Before FFCS Registration

  • Course Structure: At VIT, each course is defined using:
    • T - Theory
    • P - Project
    • L - Lab
    Please check all components carefully (e.g., T/P/L) before choosing a course.
  • Project Registration: If you choose a project-based course (P), you must complete the work under the guidance of the specific faculty you selected at the time of registration.
  • Slot Selection Tips:
    • You can assign theory in the morning (e.g., A1, B1) and lab in the afternoon (e.g., A2, B2).
    • Or vice versa — theory in the afternoon and lab in the morning — depending on availability and convenience.
    • This flexibility helps reduce clashes and keeps your day balanced.
  • Credit Limits: You must register for a minimum of 16 credits and a maximum of 27 credits. Choose wisely based on your workload.
  • Course Prerequisites: Ensure you have completed any required prerequisites before selecting electives or advanced courses.
  • Time Table Review: After registration, check your timetable in VTOP to make sure there are no slot clashes or scheduling errors.
  • Next Page Features:
    • You can select multiple slot options (e.g., A1, B1, etc.) for each course.
    • You can also delete the last added course block before generating the timetable.
  • Lab Slot Pairs: If your course includes a lab, you’ll be able to select from valid lab pairs such as L2+L3, L4+L5, or dynamically generated pairs like L31+L32. Only valid combinations will appear based on availability.
⚠️ Once submitted, courses cannot be changed unless during the add/drop period. Plan ahead and avoid last-minute confusion!
